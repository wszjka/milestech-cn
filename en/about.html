Company Overview

Shijiazhuang Miles Technology Co., Ltd. is a global trading company specializing in automation equipment. With the group's 20+ years of expertise in automation equipment and steel trading, we possess deep understanding of customized machinery — laying a solid foundation for delivering durable and reliable packaging equipment.

We are committed to providing global clients with high-quality packaging machinery and professional customized solutions. Leveraging the group's deep industry resources and global supply chain advantages, we precisely match client needs with one-stop service — from equipment selection and technical consulting to after-sales support.
